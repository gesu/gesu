Books

I like reading other peoples' book lists, so here's mine. A digital bookshelf isn't quite as nice as a physical one, but it is much lighter. I believe that what you read, along with your own personal experiences at that particular time, influence your perception and mental model of the world a great deal even if you're not aware of it. This means that rereading stuff isn't necessarily a waste of time because even if the text has changed, the context that you're consuming the work has.
